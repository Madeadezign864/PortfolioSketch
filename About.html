What are bricks made of?
Some bricks are made of crushed cinders. Crushed cinders can be mixed with cement to form strong solid bricks. Cinders can be collected in quantity from blast furnaces.In the past cinders could be obtained from steam engines.
What are bricks made of?
Bricks can also be made of fired clay.These clay bricks are dried in a furnace to make them robust.Clay bricks are used for building walls, mainly for domestic housing.Making thicker walls from clay bricks however, is time consuming.




What are bricks made of?
Some bricks are made into large blocks.As these are used in building with multiple stories, these blocks are made of a strong sand/cement mixture.Blocks enable the building to be completed quicker than if just clay bricks were used.
What are bricks made of?
Bricks can also be made of concrete panels, pillars or slabs. As a lot of high rise buildings need to be extremely strong, concrete pillars are places at strategic points, to strengthen the building's walls.These are harder to fit.
